Cedar Planked Salmon

Description

Grilled salmon served with lemon slices and green onions. It looks fresh and tasty, perfect for a simple healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions
  • Fresh ginger
  • Minced garlic

Steps

  1. Soak the cedar planks.
  2. Make the marinade.
  3. Grill the planks until they start to smoke and crackle
  4. Place the salmon on the planks, close the grill, and grill until the salmon is flaky.
